Canada Study Permit Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a study permit in Canada:

1. The applicant must have a letter of acceptance from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).

2. The applicant must prove sufficient funds to pay tuition fees, living expenses, and return transportation.

3. The applicant must have no criminal record and may need to provide a police certificate.

4. The applicant must be in good health and may need to complete a medical examination.

5. The applicant must demonstrate intent to leave Canada after studies are completed.
