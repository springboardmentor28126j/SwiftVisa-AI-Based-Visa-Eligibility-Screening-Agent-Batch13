Country: Canada
Visa Type: Federal Skilled Worker Program

Official Source:
https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/immigrate-canada/express-entry/eligibility/federal-skilled-workers.html

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have at least one year of skilled work experience.
2. Must meet minimum language requirements.
3. Must have educational credential assessment.
4. Must score required points under selection factors.
5. Must demonstrate sufficient settlement funds.
