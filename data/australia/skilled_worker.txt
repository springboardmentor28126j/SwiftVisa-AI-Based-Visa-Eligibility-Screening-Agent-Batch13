Country: Australia
Visa Type: Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)

Official Source:
https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/skilled-independent-189

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have an occupation listed on the relevant skilled occupation list.
2. Must obtain a positive skills assessment.
3. Must submit an Expression of Interest (EOI).
4. Must meet the minimum points test requirement.
5. Must meet English language requirements.
6. Must satisfy health and character requirements.
