Australia Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189)

Overview:
This visa allows skilled workers to live and work permanently in Australia.

Eligibility:
- Age under 45
- Minimum 65 points in points-based immigration system
- Occupation listed in Skilled Occupation List
- Competent English proficiency
- Positive skills assessment

Documents Required:
- Passport
- Skills assessment report
- English test result
- Work experience documents
