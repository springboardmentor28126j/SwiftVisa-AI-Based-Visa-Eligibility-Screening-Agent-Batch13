Country: Australia
Visa Type: Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Official Source:
https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must be enrolled in a registered full-time course in Australia.
2. Must provide Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE).
3. Must demonstrate sufficient financial capacity to cover tuition, travel, and living expenses.
4. Must meet English language proficiency requirements.
5. Must meet health and character requirements.
6. Must satisfy Genuine Student (GS) requirement.
