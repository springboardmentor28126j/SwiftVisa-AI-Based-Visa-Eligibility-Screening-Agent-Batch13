Australia Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Overview:
Allows international students to study full-time in Australia.

Eligibility:
- Confirmation of Enrolment from Australian institution
- Proof of financial support
- English proficiency

Documents Required:
- Passport
- CoE (Confirmation of Enrolment)
- Bank statement

Official Source:
https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/
