Country: Ireland
Visa Type: Critical Skills Employment Permit

Official Source:
https://enterprise.gov.ie/en/what-we-do/workplace-and-skills/employment-permits/permit-types/critical-skills-employment-permit/

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have a job offer from an Irish employer.
2. Employment must be in an eligible occupation.
3. Must meet the minimum salary threshold.
4. Must possess required qualifications and skills.
5. Employment contract must be valid for required duration.
