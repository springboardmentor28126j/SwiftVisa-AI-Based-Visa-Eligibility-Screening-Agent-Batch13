Country: Ireland
Visa Type: Student Visa (Stamp 2)

Official Source:
https://www.irishimmigration.ie/coming-to-study-in-ireland/

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have an offer letter from a recognized Irish educational institution.
2. Must provide proof of payment of tuition fees.
3. Must demonstrate sufficient financial resources.
4. Must have private medical insurance.
5. Must show intention to leave Ireland after completion of studies.
