Country: United Arab Emirates
Visa Type: Student Residence Visa

Official Source:
https://u.ae/en/information-and-services/education/student-visa

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must be enrolled in a recognized educational institution in the UAE.
2. Must provide proof of admission.
3. Must demonstrate financial support.
4. Must pass required medical fitness test.
5. Must have sponsorship from parent or institution.
