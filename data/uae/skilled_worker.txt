Country: United Arab Emirates
Visa Type: Employment Residence Visa

Official Source:
https://u.ae/en/information-and-services/jobs/employment-visas-and-work-permits

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have a job offer from a UAE employer.
2. Employer must sponsor the visa application.
3. Must pass medical fitness examination.
4. Must obtain labor approval and work permit.
5. Must comply with UAE employment regulations.
