Country: New Zealand
Visa Type: Student Visa

Official Source:
https://www.immigration.govt.nz/new-zealand-visas/options/study

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have an offer of place from an approved New Zealand education provider.
2. Must demonstrate sufficient funds for tuition and living expenses.
3. Must provide evidence of onward travel or sufficient funds.
4. Must meet health and character requirements.
5. Must have appropriate medical and travel insurance.
