Country: New Zealand
Visa Type: Skilled Migrant Category

Official Source:
https://www.immigration.govt.nz/new-zealand-visas/options/work/skilled-migrant-category

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must meet points threshold under skilled migrant category.
2. Must have relevant skilled employment or job offer.
3. Must meet English language requirements.
4. Must satisfy health and character requirements.
5. Employment must align with recognized skill categories.
