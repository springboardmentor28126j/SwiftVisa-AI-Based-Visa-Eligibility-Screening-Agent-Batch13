Canada Express Entry is an immigration system for skilled workers.

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Age:
Applicants between 18 and 35 receive maximum CRS points.
Points decrease after age 35.

2. Education:
Minimum requirement is high school.
Higher education such as bachelor’s, master’s, or PhD increases CRS score.
Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) is required for foreign degrees.

3. Language:
Must take IELTS or CELPIP.
Minimum CLB 7 required for Federal Skilled Worker Program.

4. Work Experience:
At least 1 year of continuous skilled work experience.
Experience must be under NOC TEER category 0, 1, 2, or 3.

5. Proof of Funds:
Applicants must show sufficient settlement funds unless they have a valid job offer.

6. CRS Score:
Candidates are ranked under Comprehensive Ranking System.
Higher score increases chance of Invitation to Apply (ITA).

7. Medical and Police Clearance:
Applicants must pass medical examination and provide police clearance certificates.
