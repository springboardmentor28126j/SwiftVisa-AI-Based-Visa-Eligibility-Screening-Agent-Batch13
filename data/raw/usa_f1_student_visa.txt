The F-1 visa allows international students to study in the United States.

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Admission:
Must be accepted by a SEVP-approved US institution.
Must receive Form I-20 from the institution.

2. Financial Support:
Must show proof of sufficient funds to cover tuition and living expenses.

3. English Proficiency:
Must meet English language requirements (TOEFL, IELTS, or equivalent).

4. Full-Time Enrollment:
Student must enroll in full-time academic program.

5. Intent to Return:
Applicant must demonstrate non-immigrant intent and plan to return to home country.

6. SEVIS Fee:
Must pay SEVIS I-901 fee before visa interview.

7. Visa Interview:
Must attend interview at US embassy or consulate.
