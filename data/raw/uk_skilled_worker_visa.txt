The UK Skilled Worker visa allows foreign nationals to work in the UK.

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Job Offer:
Must have a confirmed job offer from a UK licensed sponsor.

2. Skill Level:
Job must be at required skill level (RQF Level 3 or above).

3. Salary Requirement:
Minimum salary threshold usually £26,200 per year or occupation-specific rate.

4. English Language:
Must prove English proficiency.

5. Certificate of Sponsorship:
Employer must provide Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS).

6. Financial Requirement:
Must show proof of maintenance funds unless employer certifies support.

7. Criminal Record Certificate:
Required for certain job categories.
