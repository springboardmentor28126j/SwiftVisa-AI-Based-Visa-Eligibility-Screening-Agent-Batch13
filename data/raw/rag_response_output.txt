For a United Kingdom Student Visa, the financial requirements are as follows:

**Financial Profile:**

*   **Funding Source:** Self, Sponsor, Loan
*   **Annual Income Threshold:** Minimum 15,000 GBP. This must meet UKVI tuition plus maintenance financial requirement.
*   **Liquid Savings Threshold:** Minimum 12,000 GBP. These funds must cover tuition and required living expenses.
*   **Bank Statement Consistency:** True
*   **Has Last 3 Years ITR:** False

**Official Policy Sources:**
*   https://www.gov.uk/standard-visitor
*   https://www.gov.uk/student-visa
*   https://www.gov.uk/skilled-worker-visa
