Country: Sweden
Visa Type: Work Permit

Official Source:
https://www.migrationsverket.se/English/Private-individuals/Working-in-Sweden.html

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have a valid job offer from a Swedish employer.
2. Employment must meet minimum salary and employment conditions.
3. Employer must provide necessary employment documentation.
4. Must have valid passport.
5. Must comply with Swedish labor and immigration laws.
