Country: Sweden
Visa Type: Residence Permit for Studies

Official Source:
https://www.migrationsverket.se/English/Private-individuals/Studying-in-Sweden.html

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must be admitted to a full-time higher education program in Sweden.
2. Must pay the first installment of tuition fees (if applicable).
3. Must demonstrate sufficient financial means for living expenses.
4. Must have comprehensive health insurance coverage.
5. Must hold a valid passport for the duration of stay.
