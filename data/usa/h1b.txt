Country: United States
Visa Type: H-1B Specialty Occupation Visa

Official Source:
https://www.uscis.gov/working-in-the-united-states/h-1b-specialty-occupations

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have a job offer in a specialty occupation.
2. Must hold at least a bachelor's degree or equivalent.
3. Employer must file Labor Condition Application.
4. Must meet prevailing wage requirement.
5. Employer must sponsor the petition.
