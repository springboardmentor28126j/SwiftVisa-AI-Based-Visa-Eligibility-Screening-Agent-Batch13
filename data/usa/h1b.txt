H1B Work Visa Eligibility Requirements (USA)

To qualify for an H1B visa:

1. The position must qualify as a specialty occupation requiring specialized knowledge.

2. The applicant must hold at least a bachelor's degree or equivalent in a related field.

3. A U.S. employer must sponsor the applicant.

4. The employer must file a Labor Condition Application (LCA) approved by the Department of Labor.

5. The employer must pay at least the prevailing wage for the occupation.

6. The applicant must possess required licenses or certifications if applicable.
