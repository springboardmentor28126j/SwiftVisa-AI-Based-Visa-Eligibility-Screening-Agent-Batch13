Country: United States
Visa Type: F1 Student Visa

Official Source:
https://www.uscis.gov/working-in-the-united-states/students-and-exchange-visitors/students-and-employment

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must be accepted by a SEVP-approved school.
2. Must demonstrate sufficient financial resources.
3. Must maintain full-time enrollment.
4. Must show non-immigrant intent.
5. Must possess valid academic qualifications.
