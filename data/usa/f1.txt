F1 Student Visa Eligibility Requirements (USA)

To qualify for an F1 student visa:

1. The applicant must be accepted into a SEVP-approved educational institution in the United States.

2. The applicant must demonstrate sufficient financial resources to cover tuition, living expenses, and other educational costs during the period of study.

3. The applicant must maintain full-time student enrollment.

4. The applicant must demonstrate intent to return to their home country after completion of studies (non-immigrant intent).

5. The applicant must meet English language proficiency requirements if required by the institution.

6. The applicant must possess a valid passport and necessary academic qualifications for admission.
