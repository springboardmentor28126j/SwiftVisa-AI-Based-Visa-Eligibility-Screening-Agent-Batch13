Country: Netherlands
Visa Type: Highly Skilled Migrant

Official Source:
https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/highly-skilled-migrant

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have an employment contract with a recognized sponsor.
2. Must meet the minimum salary requirement.
3. Employment must correspond to professional qualification.
4. Employer must be registered with IND.
5. Must comply with Dutch immigration regulations.
