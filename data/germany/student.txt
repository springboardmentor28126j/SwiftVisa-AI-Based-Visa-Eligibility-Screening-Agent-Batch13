Country: Germany
Visa Type: Student Visa

Official Source:
https://www.auswaertiges-amt.de/en/visa-service/studying-in-germany/207866

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have an admission letter from a recognized German university.
2. Must provide proof of sufficient financial resources (e.g., blocked account).
3. Must have valid health insurance coverage.
4. Must meet academic qualification requirements.
5. Must demonstrate purpose of stay for study.
