Country: United Kingdom
Visa Type: Student Visa

Official Source:
https://www.gov.uk/student-visa

Eligibility Requirements:

1. Must have a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from a licensed sponsor.
2. Must demonstrate sufficient funds to support stay.
3. Must meet English language requirements.
4. Must have parental consent if under 18.
5. Must meet health surcharge requirements.
