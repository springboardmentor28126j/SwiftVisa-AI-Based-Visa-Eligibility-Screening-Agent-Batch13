UK Skilled Worker Visa Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a Skilled Worker visa:

1. The applicant must have a job offer from a UK employer approved by the Home Office.

2. The employer must provide a valid Certificate of Sponsorship.

3. The job must meet the required skill level.

4. The applicant must meet the minimum salary threshold.

5. The applicant must demonstrate English language proficiency.

6. The applicant must have sufficient personal savings if required.
